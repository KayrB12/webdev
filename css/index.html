San Luis Obispo, California

San Luis Obispo California boasts it's beautiful scenery and year 'round mild weather. This beautiful county also contains both beaches and forests as well as many vineyards.

Why San Luis Obispo?

Although I have only visited this place once it quickly became one of my favorites. It was one of my most fun trips I have ever taken. This was in no small part due to it's beautiful scenery, perfect weather and many attractions.
